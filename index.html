About Us

Where curiosity meets guidance

Founded on the belief that education should inspire, Harizon Public School provides a balanced curriculum that empowers students to think critically, act with integrity, and lead with empathy.

CBSE Aligned

Structured academics with modern pedagogy.

STEM Focus

Labs and projects that build real skills.

Arts & Sports

Clubs, teams and cultural programs.

Safe Campus

CCTV, infirmary and student support.

Holistic, future-ready learning

From foundational concepts to advanced exploration, our programs scale with every learner.

Primary (I–V)

Playful exploration, language fluency, number sense, art & movement.

Middle (VI–VIII)

Integrated STEM, lab work, problem-solving, coding foundations.

Secondary (IX–XII)

Streams, labs, Olympiad prep, career & life skills, board readiness.

Facilities that empower

Smart classrooms, modern labs, well-stocked library, sports fields and creative studios—all in a green, secure campus.

  • Smart Boards & Computer Lab
  • Physics, Chemistry & Biology Labs
  • Library & Reading Room
  • Indoor Sports Arena & Playground
  • Art, Music & Dance Studios
